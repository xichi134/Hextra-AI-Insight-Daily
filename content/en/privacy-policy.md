### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking technologies to monitor activity on our Service and store certain information. The tracking tech we use includes beacons, tags, and scripts, all working to collect and track info and to improve and analyze our Service. The technologies we use may include:

-   **Cookies or Browser Cookies:** A Cookie is a tiny file placed on your Device. You can set your browser to refuse all Cookies or to alert you when a Cookie is being sent. But, if you don't accept Cookies, you might not be able to use some parts of our Service. Unless you've adjusted your browser setting to refuse Cookies, our Service may use them. They're pretty handy, but you're the boss!
-   **Web Beacons:** Certain sections of our Service and our emails might contain small electronic files known as web beacons (also called clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These allow the Company to, for example, count users who have visited those pages or opened an email, and for other related website statistics (e.g., recording the popularity of a certain section and verifying system and server integrity). They help us see what's popular!

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies stay on your personal computer or mobile device even when you go offline, while Session Cookies are deleted as soon as you close your web browser. It's like the difference between a sticky note and a quick thought.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the purposes listed below:

-   **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
    -   **Type:** Session Cookies
    -   **Administered by:** Us
    -   **Purpose:** These Cookies are vital to provide you with services available through the Website and to enable you to use some of its features. They help authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for simply can't be provided, and we only use these Cookies to give you those services. Basically, they keep things running!

-   **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
    -   **Type:** Persistent Cookies
    -   **Administered by:** Us
    -   **Purpose:** These Cookies figure out if users have accepted the use of cookies on the Website. So we know you're cool with it.

-   **Functionality Cookies**
    -   **Type:** Persistent Cookies
    -   **Administered by:** Us
    -   **Purpose:** These Cookies let us remember choices you make when you use the Website, like remembering your login details or language preference. The goal is to give you a more personalized experience and save you the hassle of re-entering your preferences every time you use the Website. Smart, right?

For more info about the Cookies we use and your choices regarding them, check out our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy. Dive in if you're curious!

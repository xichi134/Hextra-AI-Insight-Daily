### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We're big fans of Cookies and other tracking tech to keep tabs on what's happening on our Service and stash away some info. We're talking beacons, tags, and scripts—all for gathering data, tracking stuff, and generally making our Service slicker and better. Here's a peek at what we use:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: Basically, a Cookie is just a tiny file that lands on your device. You can totally tell your browser to reject all Cookies or give you a heads-up when one's coming your way. But, fair warning, if you don't play nice with Cookies, some parts of our Service might not work for you. Unless you've specifically told your browser to ditch Cookies, our Service will likely be using them.
- **Web Beacons**: Some corners of our Service and even our emails might have these tiny electronic files called web beacons (think clear GIFs, pixel tags, or single-pixel GIFs). They let us, for instance, tally up how many folks visited certain pages or opened an email, and help with other cool website stats (like seeing which parts are popular or making sure our systems are solid).

So, Cookies can be either 'Persistent' or 'Session' Cookies. The Persistent ones stick around on your computer or phone even after you log off, but Session Cookies? Poof! They're gone the moment you close your browser.

We rock both Session and Persistent Cookies for these reasons:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies** 🤩
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Managed by**: Yours truly (that's us!)
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super critical for making sure you get all the services available on the Website and can use its cool features. They're like our bouncers, helping to confirm who you are and stopping any sneaky, fraudulent account use. Without 'em, the services you're after just won't work, and honestly, we only use them to get those services to you.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Managed by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These little guys simply remember if you've already given a thumbs-up to using cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies** 🧠
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Managed by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These smart Cookies help us recall the choices you make while on the Website, like remembering your login info or what language you prefer. The whole point? To give you a super personalized experience so you don't have to keep re-typing your preferences every single time you visit.

Wanna know more about the Cookies we use and what choices you've got? Head over to our Cookies Policy or just check out the Cookies section in this very Privacy Policy.

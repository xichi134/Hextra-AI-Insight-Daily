### Tracking Tech & Cookies: What's the Deal? 🍪
We rely on Cookies and other similar tracking technologies to keep tabs on activity within our Service and store certain bits of information. The tracking tech we're using includes beacons, tags, and scripts, which help us collect and track info, plus improve and analyze our Service. Here's a quick peek at the tech we might be using:

-   **Cookies (or Browser Cookies, if you wanna be specific!)**: So, a Cookie? It's just a tiny file chilling on your Device. You can totally tell your browser to reject all Cookies or give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But, heads-up! If you don't play nice with Cookies, you might miss out on some cool parts of our Service. Unless you've adjusted your browser settings to give Cookies the boot, our Service will likely use them.
-   **Web Beacons**: Certain sections of our Service and even our emails might have these tiny electronic files called Web Beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These little guys let the Company, for instance, count who's visited those pages or opened an email. They're also super handy for other website stats, like figuring out how popular a section is or double-checking our system and server integrity.

Cookies come in two flavors: "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device even after you go offline. Session Cookies? Poof! Gone the moment you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for these awesome reasons:

-   **Necessary / Essential Cookies (The Must-Haves!)**
    -   **Type**: Session Cookies
    -   **Administered by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies are, well, essential! They're the backbone for giving you the services available through the Website and making sure you can use all its cool features. They help us confirm who you are and block any fishy business with user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you're asking for just wouldn't work, and trust us, we only use them to get you those services!

-   **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies (Got the Memo?)**
    -   **Type**: Persistent Cookies
    -   **Administered by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies simply remember if you've already given us the green light to use cookies on the Website. Easy peasy!

-   **Functionality Cookies (Making Life Easier!)**
    -   **Type**: Persistent Cookies
    -   **Administered by**: Us
    -   **Purpose**: These Cookies are super helpful! They let us remember your choices when you're rocking the Website, like keeping your login deets or language preference saved. The whole point? To give you a more personalized experience, so you don't have to re-enter your preferences every single time you visit. Sweet!

Wanna dig deeper into the Cookies we use and your options? Just hit up our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of this Privacy Policy. 📖

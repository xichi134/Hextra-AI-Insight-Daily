### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We're using Cookies and similar tracking technologies to keep tabs on activity on our Service and store certain info. The tracking tech we use includes beacons, tags, and scripts – all for collecting and tracking info, and for improving and analyzing our Service. The tech we might use includes:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a tiny file dropped onto your Device. You can tell your browser to straight-up refuse all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But hey, if you don't accept Cookies, you might not be able to use certain parts of our Service. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to reject Cookies, our Service might just use 'em.
- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and our emails might contain small electronic files called Web Beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These bad boys let the Company, for instance, count users who've visited those pages or opened an email, and for other related website stats (like noting the popularity of a certain section and double-checking system and server integrity).

Cookies can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies chill on your personal computer or mobile device even when you go offline, while Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the following reasons:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are absolutely essential to get you the services available through the Website and to let you use some of its cool features. They help authenticate users and keep fraudulent use of user accounts in check. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for can't be delivered, and we only use these Cookies to hook you up with those services.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are used to figure out if users have given the thumbs-up to the use of cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you're using the Website, like remembering your login deets or language preferences. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you the hassle of re-entering your preferences every single time you use the Website.

For even more info on the Cookies we use and your options regarding Cookies, hit up our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.

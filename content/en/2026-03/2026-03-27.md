### Cutting-Edge Research

1.  **Google's TurboQuant paper has seriously hit memory stock prices**. Google Research introduced the [TurboQuant compression algorithm (AI News)](https://research.google/blog/turboquant-redefining-ai-efficiency-with-extreme-compression/), achieving **6x compression** for KV cache with zero precision loss. Micron and Western Digital shares plummeted in response. By using **polar coordinate quantization**, it eliminates overhead, boosting attention computation speed on H100 by **8 times**. Cloudflare CEO called it [memory's DeepSeek moment (AI News)](https://www.qbitai.com/2026/03/392215.html). Yikes! 📉🤯

Its hallucination rate was even lower than a [72 billion parameter large model (AI News)](https://www.aibase.com/zh/news/26584). Crazy! 🏆👀

4.  **DarkDriving achieves centimeter-level day-night alignment**. The [night-time data challenge (AI News)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.18067) for autonomous driving has been overcome. How cool is that? 🚗💡

5.  **An end-to-end document parsing framework boosts robustness**.

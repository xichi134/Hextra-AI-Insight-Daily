### Cutting-Edge Research

1. The **IPPg framework** is slashing multimodal inference costs by 90%! Researchers have unveiled a [new paradigm for image prompt packaging (AI News)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02492) that directly embeds **structured text** into images. This beast achieves an astonishing **96%** token compression rate. 🔥 In **GPT-4.1** tests, it was a win-win for both accuracy and cost. However, Claude 3.5's performance on VQA was a bit shaky.

2. The **EyeVLA robot eye** is making active vision a reality! This brand-new embodied intelligence framework, [EyeVLA, has been released (AI News)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.15279). Robots can now 👁️ autonomously adjust their gimbals and zoom. It only takes **500 real-world samples** for training, yet it achieves a whopping **96%** task completion rate. This seriously sets a new paradigm for **active perception**.

3. A new survey on **large model plugin credential leakage** is out! A research team analyzed [170,000 Agent skill plugins (AI News)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.03070) and found **520 vulnerable skills**. Get this: print statements in debugging logs 😱 actually leaked **73.5%** of the keys! Turns out, you need **cross-modal analysis** to uncover most of these issues. They've even released a public dataset for future 🛡️ security research.

4. **Threshold logic** is finally revealing the true nature of generative AI! A new paper [reinterprets neural computation (AI News)](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.02476) from a mathematical perspective. In high-dimensional spaces, classifiers transform into 💡 **navigators**, and **network depth** is essentially a tool for deforming data manifolds. They've proposed a **holy trinity** framework of threshold, dimension, and depth. This finally gives the black box a transparent explanation!
